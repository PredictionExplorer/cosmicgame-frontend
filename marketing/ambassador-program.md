# Cosmic Signature Ambassador Program

## Program Name: "Cosmic Ambassadors"

---

## 1. Program Overview

The Cosmic Ambassadors program empowers passionate community members to represent, promote, and grow the Cosmic Signature ecosystem in their local communities and online networks. Ambassadors are the bridge between the team and the global player base.

**Program size**: 20-50 ambassadors (initial cohort)
**Duration**: 3-month terms, renewable based on performance
**Launch timing**: Week 11 of marketing calendar (Growth phase)

---

## 3. Application Process

### Eligibility Requirements

- Active participant in Cosmic Signature (at least 5 bids placed)
- Member of Discord for at least 2 weeks
- Active social media presence (any platform, 500+ followers preferred but not required)
- Clean community record (no warnings, bans, or spam history)
- Able to commit 5-10 hours per week to ambassador activities

### Application Form Fields

1. **Discord username**
2. **Wallet address** (for verification and rewards)
3. **X/Twitter handle**
4. **Other social platforms** (YouTube, TikTok, Telegram, blog, etc.)
5. **Follower count** (across all platforms)
6. **Country / region / timezone**
7. **Primary language(s)**
8. **Why do you want to be a Cosmic Ambassador?** (200 words max)
9. **What unique value can you bring?** (200 words max)
10. **Share a link to content you've created** (any topic -- shows content ability)
11. **How many hours per week can you commit?**
12. **Have you been an ambassador for other projects?** (If yes, which ones?)

### Selection Criteria (Scoring Matrix)

| Criteria             | Weight | Description                                                             |
| -------------------- | ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Community engagement | 25%    | Discord activity, helpfulness, quality of discussions                   |
| Content quality      | 25%    | Existing content samples, writing/video/art ability                     |
| Reach potential      | 20%    | Social following, regional influence, platform diversity                |
| Game knowledge       | 15%    | Understanding of Cosmic Signature mechanics (assessed from application) |
| Commitment           | 15%    | Hours available, enthusiasm, prior ambassador experience                |

### Selection Process

1. **Application open**: 2-week window
2. **Initial screening**: Filter by eligibility requirements (automated)
3. **Scoring**: Team scores applications using matrix (3-5 business days)
4. **Interview**: Top candidates get a 15-minute Discord voice chat
5. **Selection**: Final cohort announced within 1 week of interview completion
6. **Onboarding**: 1-week structured onboarding (see Section 6)

### Game Mechanics Training Curriculum (Day 2 Deep Dive)

Ambassadors must understand every game mechanic to represent the project accurately. This curriculum covers every aspect with precise, contract-sourced information.

**Module 1: The Value Proposition (15 min)**

- Zero creator ETH extraction -- no team wallet receives ETH
- Compare to typical NFT projects: creators mint, sell, keep proceeds; Cosmic Signature inverts this
- 7% to Protocol Guild every round, enforced by smart contract code
- ~50% ETH retained grows the prize pool over time
- 10+ prize categories: everyone who plays has a path to earning

**Module 2: Round Lifecycle (20 min)**

- Three stages: Inactive (30-min cooldown), Active pre-bid (Dutch auction declining), Active post-bid (timer running)
- ETH Dutch auction: ceiling = 2x previous round's first bid, floor = ceiling/200, duration ~2 days
- First bid must be ETH -- starts the timer at ~1 day initial duration
- After first bid: ETH price increases ~1% per bid (price + price/100 + 1 wei)
- Timer extends by ~1 hour per bid: max(currentDeadline, now) + increment
- Timer increment grows ~1% per round (later rounds can last longer)
- Random Walk NFT discount: 50% off any ETH bid (one-time per NFT, you keep the NFT)

**Module 3: CST Economics (15 min)**

- CST is minted by the game only -- 100 CST per bid, 1,000 CST per winner, 3,000 CST to marketing
- CST bids follow a repeating Dutch auction (~12-hour cycle, ceiling = max(2x paid, 200 CST))
- CST bid amount is **burned** (removed from supply). Bidder still gets 100 CST reward.
- If CST bid < 100 CST (auction declined low enough), bidder gains net CST
- CST powers the DAO: ERC20Votes with timestamp clock, delegatable voting
- No fixed max supply -- dynamically minted/burned by the game

**Module 4: Champion Titles (20 min)**

- **Endurance Champion**: longest single unbroken streak as last bidder in the round. Not cumulative -- one continuous stretch.
- **Chrono-Warrior**: longest continuous reign as Endurance Champion. When EC changes hands, the outgoing EC's "chrono interval" is measured. Longest chrono wins.
- EC prize: 1 Cosmic Signature NFT + 1,000 CST
- CW prize: **8% of round's ETH** + 1 CSN + 1,000 CST (most lucrative secondary prize)
- Both titles reward strategic timing, not just capital
- Final evaluation happens at claim time -- current EC/CW standings can change at the last moment

**Module 5: Complete Prize Distribution (15 min)**

- 25% ETH: Main prize (direct to claimer)
- 8% ETH: Chrono-Warrior (PrizesWallet)
- 4% ETH: 3 random bidders (PrizesWallet)
- 6% ETH: CSN stakers (staking contract, pro-rata per NFT)
- 7% ETH: Protocol Guild (direct transfer, non-reverting)
- ~50% ETH: Retained in game contract for next round
- CST: 1,000 each to main winner, last CST bidder, EC, CW, 10 bidder raffle, up to 10 RWLK staker raffle
- CSN NFTs: 1 each to same categories above (up to 24 per round)
- 3,000 CST to marketing wallet
- Claim timeout: last bidder has 1-day exclusive window; then anyone can claim
- Secondary prize timeout: 5 weeks in PrizesWallet before anyone can withdraw

**Module 6: Staking (10 min)**

- CSN Staking: lock NFTs in staking contract. 6% of round ETH split equally per staked NFT. Cumulative rewards paid on unstake. Each NFT stakeable only once ever.
- RWLK Staking: lock NFTs for raffle eligibility. Up to 10 winners per round get 1 CSN + 1,000 CST. Each RWLK stakeable only once ever.
- Key distinction: CSN staking = passive ETH yield. RWLK staking = raffle eligibility (no direct ETH).

**Module 7: DAO Governance (10 min)**

- OpenZeppelin Governor with GovernorVotes, GovernorCountingSimple, GovernorSettings
- 100 CST proposal threshold (one bid's reward)
- 2-day voting delay, 2-week voting period, 3% quorum
- Can adjust: prize percentages, public goods recipient, timer settings, auction parameters
- Marketing wallet CST is not supposed to vote (why quorum is 3% instead of typical 4%)

**Module 8: Key Talking Points (10 min)**

- Always use "public goods funding" not "charity" in external messaging
- Emphasize zero creator ETH extraction vs typical NFT projects
- Growing prize pool: ~50% retention means prizes get bigger over time
- Formally verified (Certora) + CC0 open source -- anyone can audit
- On Arbitrum: sub-cent gas fees make the game accessible to everyone
- Random Walk NFT integration rewards an existing community with real utility
- Claim race: unclaimed main prizes after 1 day are open to anyone (opportunity for alert players)

**Module 9: Art Generation -- The Three Body Problem (15 min)**

- Every CSN NFT has a unique on-chain seed. The seed is the sole input to a deterministic art generation pipeline.
- The art visualizes the **Three Body Problem**: three massive celestial bodies orbiting under Newtonian gravity. Unlike two bodies (neat ellipses), three bodies produce fundamentally chaotic trajectories.
- **Pipeline stages (simplified)**: Seed -> SHA3-256 RNG -> 100,000 three-body simulations -> Borda selection (best orbit) -> spectral rendering (16 wavelength bins) -> cinematic post-processing -> 16-bit PNG + 30s video
- **Physics**: 4th-order Yoshida symplectic integrator (from astrophysics research). Preserves energy over millions of timesteps. G = 9.8. Bodies have mass 100-300, positions in 3D space.
- **Selection**: Orbits scored on chaos (FFT analysis) and equilateralness (triangle balance). Equilateralness dominates (~15x weight). The most visually interesting orbit wins.
- **Spectral rendering**: NOT standard RGB. 16 bins from 380 nm (violet) to 700 nm (red). Produces physically correct color mixing and transitions impossible in RGB.
- **Post-effects**: AgX tonemapping (from film pipelines), bloom, nebula clouds (OpenSimplex noise), cinematic color grading, chromatic dispersion, atmospheric depth.
- **Key talking points for art**:
  - "No AI" -- zero neural networks, diffusion models, or training data. Pure numerical computation.
  - "Deterministic" -- same seed always produces identical output, verified by SHA-256 hash in CI.
  - "Physics-based" -- real gravity, real spectral optics, not random noise or hand-drawn assets.
  - "Reproducible" -- CC0 code, anyone can verify any NFT's art independently.
  - "Museum-quality" -- 16-bit color depth, 4K-capable, H.265 10-bit video, film-grade tonemapping.
- **Common questions to prepare for**:
  - "Is it AI art?" -- No. No ML anywhere. Gravitational physics + spectral optics + signal processing.
  - "Why the Three Body Problem?" -- It produces beautiful, unpredictable trajectories that are deterministic but practically impossible to predict. Every seed creates a unique dance.
  - "Can I reproduce the art?" -- Yes. Open-source code, deterministic pipeline, CC0 license. Just need the seed.
- For full technical details, reference [art-algorithm-guide.md](art-algorithm-guide.md).

## 7. Content Guidelines for Ambassadors

### Do's

- Share genuine enthusiasm and personal experience with the game
- Educate your audience on mechanics before promoting
- Use official brand assets and approved messaging
- Disclose your ambassador status ("Cosmic Signature Ambassador")
- Report community sentiment honestly, including criticism
- Engage positively with other projects in the Arbitrum ecosystem
- Ask the team if unsure about any claim or statement

### Don'ts

- Never make price predictions or financial promises about CST
- Never share insider information before public release
- Never attack, disparage, or FUD competitor projects
- Never use misleading statistics or fake engagement
- Never spam communities or use automated posting tools
- Never represent personal opinions as official project positions
- Never share private team communications publicly

### Disclosure Template

All public ambassador content should include one of:

```
Cosmic Signature Ambassador | Views are my own
```

or

```
Disclosure: I am a Cosmic Signature Ambassador and receive CST rewards
for community contributions.
```

## 8. Performance Reviews and Tier Movement

### Monthly Review Process

1. **Self-assessment**: Ambassador submits monthly activity report with metrics
2. **Manager review**: Program manager evaluates against responsibilities and points
3. **Peer input**: Optional feedback from fellow ambassadors
4. **Tier decision**: Maintain, promote, or flag for improvement plan

### Promotion Criteria

**Tier 3 to Tier 2**:

- Minimum 2 months as Tier 3
- Average performance bonus > 60% of maximum
- At least one long-form content piece
- Active and helpful in Discord daily
- No community guidelines violations

**Tier 2 to Tier 1**:

- Minimum 3 months as Tier 2
- Managed a regional community successfully
- Created 4+ high-quality long-form content pieces
- Referred 20+ new community members
- Demonstrated leadership and reliability

### Off-boarding

Ambassadors may be removed for:

- Violating content guidelines (especially price speculation, FUD, or spam)
- Consistently missing weekly minimums (2+ consecutive weeks)
- Community guidelines violations
- Inactivity for 2+ weeks without prior notice

Off-boarding process:

1. Warning + improvement plan (1 week)
2. Final warning if not improved
3. Removal with grace period for pending rewards
