# Cosmic Signature Brand Identity Guide

## Brand Essence

Cosmic Signature is the strategic on-chain game where every round creates winners and funds Ethereum's future. Our brand communicates **strategic depth**, **cosmic wonder**, **community empowerment**, and **transparent impact**. 7% of every round goes to [Protocol Guild](https://www.protocolguild.org/) -- the collective funding mechanism for Ethereum's core protocol contributors. The more successful Cosmic Signature becomes, the more resources flow to the people building the infrastructure we all depend on.

---

## 1. Logo System

### Primary Logo

- **Wordmark**: "COSMIC SIGNATURE" in custom-spaced uppercase with a stylized "C" and "S" monogram
- The "C" forms a partial orbit ring; the "S" integrates a subtle upward trajectory (like a bid price chart)
- A single star/node accent sits at the junction of the orbit ring

### Logo Variations

| Variation                   | Usage                                        |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Full wordmark + monogram    | Website headers, press materials, documents  |
| Monogram only (C-orbit + S) | Social profile pictures, favicons, app icons |
| Horizontal lockup           | Navigation bars, partner listings            |
| Stacked lockup              | Square placements, merchandise               |

### Clear Space

- Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "C" monogram on all sides
- Never place the logo on busy backgrounds without a solid backing container

### Logo Don'ts

- Do not stretch, rotate, or skew the logo
- Do not change individual letter colors
- Do not add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
- Do not place on low-contrast backgrounds without the backing container

## 3. Typography

### Primary Typeface: Space Grotesk

- **Usage**: Headlines, navigation, UI labels, buttons
- **Weights**: Medium (500), Bold (700)
- **Why**: Geometric, modern, subtly futuristic without being illegible. Excellent screen readability.
- **Source**: Google Fonts (free, open-source)

### Secondary Typeface: Inter

- **Usage**: Body text, descriptions, long-form content, data tables
- **Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600)
- **Why**: Best-in-class screen readability at all sizes. Clean, professional, universally legible.
- **Source**: Google Fonts (free, open-source)

### Monospace Typeface: JetBrains Mono

- **Usage**: Contract addresses, transaction hashes, code snippets, ETH/CST values
- **Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500)
- **Why**: Built for code readability. Distinguishes similar characters (0/O, 1/l/I).
- **Source**: Google Fonts (free, open-source)

## 4. Tone of Voice

### Brand Personality

Cosmic Signature speaks like a **strategic advisor who genuinely cares** -- knowledgeable, confident, inclusive, and purposeful. We are never arrogant, never hype-driven, and never exclusionary.

### Voice Principles

**1. Strategic, Not Speculative**

- YES: "Time your bid during the Dutch auction decline for maximum value."
- NO: "This token is going to the moon!"

**2. Inclusive, Not Gatekeeping**

- YES: "Everyone who participates has a chance to win -- not just the last bidder."
- NO: "Only the most degen players will understand this alpha."

**3. Purposeful, Not Preachy**

- YES: "7% of every round funds Protocol Guild -- the people building Ethereum itself."
- NO: "We are saving the world through blockchain technology."

**4. Clear, Not Jargon-Heavy**

- YES: "Bid with ETH or CST. The last bidder when the timer runs out wins the main prize."
- NO: "Utilize the dual-asset bidding mechanism to interface with the time-decay oracle."

**5. Confident, Not Aggressive**

- YES: "The flagship strategy game on Arbitrum."
- NO: "We're destroying all other blockchain games."

### Tone by Context

| Context             | Tone                               | Example                                                                              |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Educational content | Clear, patient, structured         | "Here's how the Dutch auction works, step by step..."                                |
| Social media        | Energetic, concise, engaging       | "Round 47 just ended. 3.2 ETH to the winner. 0.7 ETH to Protocol Guild. Who's next?" |
| Strategy guides     | Analytical, insightful, peer-level | "The optimal bid timing depends on three variables..."                               |
| Press / PR          | Professional, factual, impactful   | "Cosmic Signature has distributed X ETH in prizes across Y rounds..."                |
| Community / Discord | Friendly, casual, celebratory      | "Congrats to the new Endurance Champion! That was a legendary streak."               |
| Error / support     | Empathetic, solution-oriented      | "Transaction didn't go through? Here's what likely happened and how to fix it."      |

## 5. Visual Language

### Photography / Imagery Style

- **No stock photography**. All imagery should be generated, illustrated, or data-driven.
- Cosmic/space themes: nebulae, star fields, orbital paths, cosmic dust
- Abstract generative art that reflects the procedural NFT generation
- Data visualizations as art (round statistics, bid timelines, prize flows)

### Iconography

- **Style**: Outlined, 2px stroke, rounded corners
- **Grid**: 24x24px base grid
- **Colors**: Stellar White on dark backgrounds; Cosmic Indigo on light
- **Key icons needed**: Bid (gavel/hammer), Timer (clock with cosmic ring), Prize (crystal/gem), Stake (lock with star), DAO (vote/governance), Public Goods (Ethereum diamond with orbit), NFT (hexagonal frame), ETH (diamond), CST (custom token icon)

### Motion & Animation Principles

- **Entrances**: Fade up with slight scale (0.95 to 1.0), 300-500ms
- **Transitions**: Smooth easing (ease-out for entrances, ease-in-out for state changes)
- **Cosmic particles**: Subtle floating particles on backgrounds (slow, ambient)
- **Countdown**: Pulsing glow effect as timer approaches zero
- **Prize moments**: Celebratory particle burst with gold/cyan accents
- **Loading states**: Orbital ring spinner (matches monogram orbit)

### Layout Principles

- **Generous whitespace**: Let content breathe against cosmic backgrounds
- **Card-based UI**: Rounded corners (12-16px radius), subtle borders or glow effects
- **Asymmetric grids**: Break from rigid columns for hero sections and feature highlights
- **Z-depth**: Use subtle shadows and glow to create layered cosmic depth
- **Mobile-first**: All layouts designed for mobile, then expanded for desktop

## 6. Social Media Visual Templates

### X/Twitter

- **Profile picture**: Monogram on Cosmic Indigo circle
- **Banner**: Deep Space gradient with tagline and key visual
- **Post templates**: Dark cards with accent borders for announcements, stats, recaps
- **Thread openers**: Bold statement + visual hook in first image

### Discord

- **Server icon**: Monogram on Cosmic Indigo
- **Banner**: Extended cosmic scene with game elements
- **Role colors**: Match brand palette (Nebula Violet for mods, Solar Gold for champions, etc.)
- **Embed colors**: Nebula Violet for general, Solar Gold for prizes, Impact Green for public goods / Protocol Guild

### General Social

- **Aspect ratios**: 1:1 for profile pics, 16:9 for banners, 1.91:1 for X cards, 9:16 for Stories
- **Text overlay**: Maximum 20% of image area for text
- **Consistent framing**: All social images use the same dark background treatment
