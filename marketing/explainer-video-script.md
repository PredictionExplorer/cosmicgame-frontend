# Cosmic Signature Explainer Video Script

## Production Specifications

| Spec              | Value                                                                                                         |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Duration**      | 100-125 seconds                                                                                               |
| **Style**         | 2D motion graphics with cosmic/space aesthetic                                                                |
| **Resolution**    | 1920x1080 (16:9) + 1080x1080 (1:1 social cut) + 1080x1920 (9:16 Stories cut)                                  |
| **Color Palette** | Per brand identity guide (Cosmic Indigo, Nebula Violet, Solar Gold, Aurora Cyan, Chrono Rose, Impact Green)   |
| **Typography**    | Space Grotesk (headlines), Inter (body), JetBrains Mono (numbers/data)                                        |
| **Music**         | Ambient electronic with subtle cosmic undertones. Builds tension toward "claim" moment. Uplifting resolution. |
| **Voiceover**     | Professional, clear, confident. Gender-neutral preferred. Moderate pace (~150 WPM).                           |

---

## Storyboard and Script

### SCENE 1: The Problem (0:00 - 0:10)

**VISUAL**: Dark space. Ghostly outlines of typical NFT projects float by -- mint buttons, royalty flows, arrows pointing to a "TEAM WALLET." The value flows one way. Fades to darkness.

**VOICEOVER**:

> "Most NFT projects take your money and send it to the team. Floor goes up? Maybe. Floor goes down? Probably. Either way, creators win and you hope for the best."

**TEXT ON SCREEN**: (none -- pure visual storytelling)

---

### SCENE 2: The Inversion (0:10 - 0:22)

**VISUAL**: The Cosmic Signature logo assembles from cosmic dust, shattering the old model. A flow diagram appears showing ETH flowing FROM the game TO players, stakers, public goods -- with "0% to Team" prominently displayed. Arbitrum logo glows subtly in the background.

**VOICEOVER**:

> "Cosmic Signature inverts this completely. Zero ETH goes to any team wallet. Every wei goes to players, stakers, and public goods. This is a game built for you."

**TEXT ON SCREEN**: "COSMIC SIGNATURE" / "Zero ETH to Creators" / "Built on Arbitrum"

---

### SCENE 3: How It Works (0:22 - 0:42)

**VISUAL**: A circular game arena appears with a countdown timer at center. A price chart shows the ETH Dutch auction -- starting at a ceiling, declining linearly over ~2 days. Someone bids: the price jumps slightly (+1%) and the timer extends. A second lane labeled "CST" shows its own Dutch auction with "BURNED" text appearing when someone bids with CST. A "100 CST" reward floats to each bidder.

**VOICEOVER**:

> "Each round starts with a Dutch auction -- the ETH bid price starts high and drops over two days until someone bids. After that, each bid costs about one percent more and extends the countdown timer by about one hour. You can also bid with CST tokens -- those are burned from supply. And every single bid, ETH or CST, instantly earns you one hundred CST."

**TEXT ON SCREEN**: "Dutch Auction: Fair Price Discovery" / "+1% Per Bid" / "100 CST Every Bid"

---

### SCENE 3.5: The Art (0:42 - 0:52)

**VISUAL**: The screen transitions to deep space. Three glowing bodies appear -- orbiting each other under gravity. Their paths leave spectral light trails that accumulate into a complex, beautiful pattern. A seed hex value (`0x46205528`) pulses in the corner. Text overlays fade in: "Three Body Problem" / "Real Physics" / "No AI." The visualization morphs between several different seed outputs, each visually distinct.

**VOICEOVER**:

> "And the art? It's not AI-generated. Each NFT is born from the Three Body Problem -- three celestial bodies simulated under real Newtonian gravity. One hundred thousand orbits evaluated. The best one rendered in spectral light across sixteen wavelength bins. Same seed, same art, every time. Physics as the artist."

**TEXT ON SCREEN**: "Three Body Problem" / "100,000 Orbits" / "Spectral Rendering" / "No AI"

---

### SCENE 4: Champion Titles (1:05 - 1:18)

**VISUAL**: Two player avatars are highlighted in the arena. A clock shows one player holding "last bidder" status for a long unbroken stretch -- a glowing "ENDURANCE CHAMPION" crown appears above them. A second, meta-layer shows who held the EC crown longest -- the "CHRONO-WARRIOR" lightning bolt appears. Both avatars receive prizes.

**VOICEOVER**:

> "But it's not just about having the most ETH. Strategic timing earns you champion titles. Hold the longest unbroken streak as last bidder and you become the Endurance Champion. Hold that crown the longest and you become the Chrono-Warrior -- earning eight percent of the entire round's ETH. Skill beats capital."

**TEXT ON SCREEN**: "Endurance Champion: Longest Streak" / "Chrono-Warrior: 8% ETH"

---

### SCENE 5: 10+ Winners (1:18 - 1:38)

**VISUAL**: The timer hits zero. A constellation of prize categories explodes outward like a supernova. Icons arrange into a grid, each pulsing as mentioned:

1. Crown (Main Prize: 25% ETH + NFT + 1,000 CST) -- gold glow
2. Shield (Endurance Champion: NFT + 1,000 CST) -- cyan glow
3. Lightning (Chrono-Warrior: 8% ETH + NFT + 1,000 CST) -- rose glow
4. Token (Last CST Bidder: NFT + 1,000 CST) -- violet glow
5. Dice (ETH Raffle: 4% split among 3 random bidders) -- gold sparkle
6. Gem (NFT Raffle: 10 random bidders + up to 10 RWLK stakers) -- cyan sparkle
7. Stack (CSN Staking: 6% ETH to staked NFT holders) -- violet glow
8. Ethereum diamond (Protocol Guild: 7% to Ethereum devs) -- green glow
9. Arrow (50% rolls to next round) -- subtle glow

**VOICEOVER**:

> "When the timer runs out, prizes go everywhere. Twenty-five percent ETH to the last bidder. Eight percent to the Chrono-Warrior. Four percent split among three random raffle winners. Six percent to Cosmic Signature NFT stakers as passive yield. Seven percent straight to Protocol Guild -- funding the one hundred seventy plus developers who build Ethereum. Plus NFTs and CST prizes to the Endurance Champion, the last CST bidder, twenty random raffle winners, and a marketing allocation. Over ten categories of winners. Every single round."

**TEXT ON SCREEN**: "10+ Prize Categories Every Round" / "Zero to Team"

---

### SCENE 6: Staking & Growth (1:38 - 1:53)

**VISUAL**: Three orbiting rings appear:

- Inner ring (gold): "EARN" -- CST tokens flowing to players with each bid
- Middle ring (cyan): "STAKE" -- Cosmic Signature NFTs locked into a staking vault, ETH rewards (6%) flowing back. Random Walk NFTs staked separately, raffle tickets floating out.
- Outer ring (violet): "GOVERN" -- DAO ballot box, proposals floating by (100 CST threshold, 2-week voting)

Then: a prize pool visualization shows Round 1 ending -- 50% distributed, 50% rolling into Round 2. Round 2's pool is bigger. Round 3 even bigger. The pool grows like a rising tide.

**VOICEOVER**:

> "Stake your Cosmic Signature NFTs for passive ETH yield from six percent of every round. Stake Random Walk NFTs for raffle eligibility. Use your CST to vote in the DAO and shape the game's parameters. And fifty percent of each round's ETH rolls into the next -- the longer the game runs, the bigger the prizes get. A self-growing, self-sustaining game economy."

**TEXT ON SCREEN**: "Stake for Yield" / "Govern with CST" / "50% Rolls Forward"

---

### SCENE 7: Call to Action (1:53 - 2:05)

**VISUAL**: All elements converge into the Cosmic Signature logo mark. Key facts appear below: "CC0 Open Source" / "Formally Verified" / "On Arbitrum." The tagline animates in. Landing page URL and social icons fade in. A "Get Notified" button pulses with the Nebula Glow gradient.

**VOICEOVER**:

> "Cosmic Signature. Open source. Formally verified. Zero ETH to creators. Bid. Endure. Win. Give. Join at cosmic signature dot com."

**TEXT ON SCREEN**: "Bid. Endure. Win. Give." / "cosmicsignature.com" / Social icons (Twitter, Discord)

## Production Notes

### Animation Timing

- Use ease-out curves for entrances (elements arriving)
- Use ease-in-out for transitions between scenes
- 0.5s crossfade between major scenes
- Micro-animations on data/numbers (counter effect for "10+", "7%", "50%", "0%")
- Price chart animation in Scene 3 should feel dynamic -- declining curve, then step-ups

### Sound Design

- Subtle "whoosh" on bid appearances
- Clock ticking during timer sequences (accelerating as it counts down)
- Celebratory chime/shimmer when prizes distribute
- Warm ambient pad throughout
- Music builds from Scene 1 to Scene 5 (the climactic prize explosion), then settles for Scenes 6-7
- "Shatter" sound effect in Scene 2 when old model breaks

### Key Visual Moments for Thumbnail/Social Cuts

1. **1:18** -- The supernova prize explosion (best for YouTube thumbnail)
2. **0:10** -- Logo reveal / model inversion (best for brand recognition)
3. **0:42** -- Three Body Problem art reveal (best for visual impact -- unique differentiator)
4. **1:05** -- Champion titles reveal (best for engagement -- aspirational)
5. **1:53** -- Final logo + tagline (best for end card)

### Deliverables

- Full 16:9 video (YouTube, website)
- 1:1 square cut, 60 seconds max (Twitter, Instagram feed)
- 9:16 vertical cut, 60 seconds max (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts)
- 15-second teaser cut (ads, pre-roll)
- 30-second summary cut
- GIF of prize explosion moment (Discord, Twitter)
- Static thumbnail (YouTube, social)

### Recommended Tools / Vendors

- **Animation**: After Effects, Rive, or Lottie-based
- **Alternative**: AI-assisted video generation (Runway, Pika) for rapid prototyping
- **Sound**: Artlist or Epidemic Sound for licensed music
- **Voiceover**: Professional VO via Voices.com, Fiverr Pro, or in-house
