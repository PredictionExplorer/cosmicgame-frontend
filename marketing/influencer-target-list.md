## Engagement Strategy by Tier

### Tier 1 (Macro): Partnership Model

- **Timeline**: Begin outreach Weeks 2-3, confirm by Week 4, publish during launch week
- **Deliverable**: Dedicated video, podcast episode, or long-form sponsored content
- **Briefing**: Full press kit + private demo + 1-on-1 walkthrough with team
- **KPI**: Views, unique visitors to site, Discord joins
- **Payment**: Fixed fee + potential CST allocation for ongoing alignment

### Tier 2 (Mid): Campaign Model

- **Timeline**: Begin outreach Week 3, stagger content across Weeks 5-8
- **Deliverable**: Twitter thread (5-10 tweets), newsletter mention, or tutorial
- **Briefing**: Press kit + strategy guide + key talking points doc
- **KPI**: Impressions, engagement rate, click-through to dApp
- **Payment**: Fixed fee per deliverable

### Tier 3 (Micro): Organic + Seeding Model

- **Timeline**: Ongoing from Week 4
- **Deliverable**: Organic reviews, reaction content, community mentions
- **Briefing**: Press kit + demo walkthrough + community invite
- **KPI**: Genuine community growth, qualitative engagement
- **Payment**: Small fixed fee, CST incentive, or organic engagement

### Tier 4-5 (Podcasts + YouTube): Content Model

- **Timeline**: Begin pitching Week 3, schedule for Weeks 6-12 rollout
- **Deliverable**: Full episode or dedicated segment
- **Briefing**: Full media kit + prepared talking points + game demo
- **KPI**: Downloads/views, social mentions post-episode

## Game Mechanics Talking Points for Influencer Briefings

Provide these accurate, contract-sourced details to all influencer partners. Every number comes from the smart contracts.

### The "Zero Creator ETH" Pitch

- No team wallet receives ETH. This is verifiable on-chain.
- The only non-participant allocation is CST tokens (not ETH) to a marketing wallet
- Marketing wallet's CST is governed by the DAO -- the community appoints the treasurer
- Compare: typical NFT projects send 100% of mint revenue to creators. Cosmic Signature sends 0%.

### The Prize Math (10 ETH Round Example)

- 2.5 ETH to the last bidder (25%)
- 0.8 ETH to the Chrono-Warrior (8%)
- 0.4 ETH split among 3 random bidders (4%)
- 0.6 ETH to CSN NFT stakers (6%, split pro-rata per staked NFT)
- 0.7 ETH to Protocol Guild (7%)
- 5.0 ETH stays for the next round (~50%)
- Plus: up to 24 Cosmic Signature NFTs minted and thousands of CST distributed

### Dutch Auction Mechanics (Unique Selling Point)

- ETH bid price starts at 2x the previous round's first bid and declines to floor over ~2 days
- After first bid: each ETH bid costs only ~1% more (price/100 + 1 wei)
- CST bids follow their own ~12-hour Dutch auction, reset after each bid (ceiling = 2x paid, min 200 CST)
- Random Walk NFT holders get 50% off any ETH bid (one-time per NFT)

### Champion Titles (Game Theory Angle)

- Endurance Champion: longest single unbroken streak as last bidder (rewards patience during quiet periods)
- Chrono-Warrior: longest reign as EC (the most lucrative secondary prize -- 8% of round ETH)
- Both reward strategic timing, not just capital. A small bidder who times a quiet period can beat whales.

### Growing Prize Pool

- ~50% of ETH stays in the game after each round
- New bids add fresh ETH, but only half leaves -- compounding effect
- The longer the game runs, the bigger the prizes naturally become
- Anyone can donate ETH to the game at any time, further growing the pool

### Security and Trust Points

- Certora formal verification (mathematical proof, not just testing)
- CC0 1.0 license (public domain -- anyone can read and audit every line)
- OpenZeppelin contract foundations (battle-tested ERC-20, ERC-721, Governor)
- UUPS upgrades only during inactive rounds (no mid-game logic changes)
- Built on Arbitrum (Ethereum L2 security with sub-cent fees)
