Anatomy of a Round

Every round of Cosmic Signature follows the same lifecycle. Here's what happens from start to finish.

PHASE 01 Round Activation

After a 30-minute cooldown following the previous round, the new round becomes active. No bids have been placed yet. The ETH Dutch auction begins its ~2-day price decline from ceiling to floor.

ETH Price
Starts at 2x previous round's first bid price, declines linearly to floor (ceiling / 200) over ~2 days
CST Price
Not available until first ETH bid is placed
Timer
Not started -- waiting for first bid
RWLK Discount
Random Walk NFT holders can bid at 50% off the current price (one-time per NFT)
Strategy
Bidding early costs more but starts the round on your terms. Waiting gets a better price but risks someone else bidding first.
PHASE 02 First Bid (Round Starts)

The first bid must be in ETH. It starts the countdown timer (~1 day initial duration), sets the ETH Dutch auction beginning price for the next round, and switches ETH pricing to incremental mode.

Requirement
First bid must be ETH (CST bids open after)
Timer Starts
Set to ~1 day from now (initial duration)
CST Reward
100 CST minted instantly to the bidder
ETH Pricing Shifts
Dutch auction ends. Next ETH bid = paidPrice + paidPrice/100 + 1 wei (~1% more)
CST Auction Opens
CST Dutch auction begins: ceiling of 200 CST (min), declining over ~12 hours
PHASE 03 Active Bidding

Players compete by placing bids in ETH or CST. Each bid extends the timer by ~1 hour. Champions are tracked in real time. Every bid earns 100 CST. Bid messages (up to 280 bytes) can accompany any bid.

ETH Bids
Price increments ~1%: previous + previous/100 + 1 wei. RWLK holders pay half.
CST Bids
Dutch auction resets per bid (ceiling = 2x paid, min 200 CST). Paid CST is burned. +100 CST reward.
Timer Extension
Each bid extends deadline by ~1 hour (grows ~1% per round). max(deadline, now) + increment.
Endurance Champion
Longest single unbroken streak as last bidder in the round. Prize: 1 CSN + 1,000 CST.
Chrono-Warrior
Longest reign as Endurance Champion. Prize: 8% ETH + 1 CSN + 1,000 CST.
Donations
Bids can include donated ERC-20 tokens or ERC-721 NFTs, added to PrizesWallet for the winner.
PHASE 04 Timer Expires

When no new bid arrives before the deadline, the timer expires. The last bidder has a 1-day exclusive window to claim. After that, anyone can claim and receive the main prize themselves.

Last Bidder
Can call claimMainPrize() immediately after timer expires
Claim Timeout
After 1 day, anyone can claim and receive the main prize
Champions Finalized
Final EC and CW evaluated at the moment of claim -- ongoing streaks are counted
Prizes Never Lock
Secondary prizes in PrizesWallet have a 5-week withdrawal window, then claimable by anyone
PHASE 05 Prize Distribution

The contract snapshots its total ETH balance, distributes percentages, mints CST and Cosmic Signature NFTs to all winner categories, and initializes the next round's inactive stage.

Main Prize
25% ETH (direct transfer) + 1 CSN + 1,000 CST to claimer
Chrono-Warrior
8% ETH (PrizesWallet) + 1 CSN + 1,000 CST
Endurance Champion
1 CSN + 1,000 CST (minted directly)
Last CST Bidder
1 CSN + 1,000 CST (minted directly)
ETH Raffles
4% ETH split among 3 random bidders (PrizesWallet)
NFT Raffle (Bidders)
10 random bidders each get 1 CSN + 1,000 CST
NFT Raffle (RWLK Stakers)
Up to 10 random RWLK stakers each get 1 CSN + 1,000 CST
CSN Stakers
6% ETH to staking contract, split equally per staked NFT
Protocol Guild
7% ETH sent directly (non-reverting -- does not block claim on failure)
Marketing Wallet
3,000 CST minted (no ETH)
Rollover
~50% ETH stays in the game contract, growing the future prize pool
