Game Mechanics

Built for Strategic Depth

Every mechanic creates meaningful decisions. Timing, economics, and game theory drive outcomes.

📈 Dual Dutch Auctions

ETH bids start with a ~2-day Dutch auction that declines to a floor, then switch to ~1% incremental steps after the first bid. CST bids follow their own ~12-hour Dutch auction that resets after each bid, with prices burned from supply. Timing is everything.

🎨 Generative NFTs with On-Chain Seeds

Every Cosmic Signature NFT carries a unique seed stored permanently on-chain, generated from block data and Arbitrum precompiles. The art is procedurally generated from this seed. You can name your NFTs (up to 32 characters). Stake them for 6% ETH yield per round.

🏠 DAO Governance

CST is your voting power through an on-chain OpenZeppelin Governor. Propose changes with just 100 CST (one bid's reward). Vote on prize percentages, public goods recipient, timer settings, and more. 2-day voting delay, 2-week voting period, 3% quorum.

🔗 Random Walk NFT Utility

Hold a Random Walk NFT? Attach it to any ETH bid for a 50% discount (one-time per NFT, you keep the NFT). Or stake it for eligibility in the per-round CSN + CST raffle (up to 10 winners). A partner collection with tangible, meaningful utility.

💪 Self-Growing Prize Pool

50% of the game's ETH stays in the contract after each round. As new bids add ETH and only half leaves, the pool compounds. Early rounds seed later ones. The longer the game runs, the bigger the prizes become. ETH donations from anyone further grow the pool.

🔒 Formally Verified & CC0

Smart contracts are verified with Certora formal verification (mathematical proof of correctness), analyzed with Slither, and built on OpenZeppelin's battle-tested foundations. Everything is CC0 licensed -- fully open source, public domain. Read every line yourself.

Art & Technology

Powered by Physics

Every NFT's artwork is generated by simulating the Three Body Problem -- three celestial bodies locked in gravitational orbit. Real physics. Spectral light. No AI.

🌏

Simulation

100,000 three-body orbits simulated with a 4th-order Yoshida symplectic integrator from astrophysics research

🏆

Selection

Borda rank aggregation selects the most visually interesting orbit via FFT chaos analysis and equilateralness scoring

🌈

Spectral Light

16 wavelength bins (380-700 nm) render the full visible spectrum with anti-aliased trails and velocity-dependent HDR

🎥

Cinema-Grade Output

AgX tonemapping, bloom, nebula overlays, cinematic grading. 16-bit PNG + 30s H.265 video at 60 fps

Same seed = same art, every time. Deterministic. Reproducible. Open source (CC0). No AI.
